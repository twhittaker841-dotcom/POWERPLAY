# POWERPLAY
i am coding a game called POWERPLAY
The Game Objective
Welcome to **POWERPLAY**. You take control of a **real-world country today**, and from that moment, the timeline becomes an **alternate history** shaped entirely by your strategic decisions. Your ultimate goal is to **dominate** the global map or achieve a specific victory condition over your rivals.

Managing Your Nation
Success hinges on the effective management of your country's core attributes:

**Economy & Resources:** Generate **Income** and manage vital **Resources** (Oil, Metal, Food) to fund all domestic projects and military expansion.
**Military Strength:** Grow your forces to defend your borders and project **Power** globally.
**Strategic Decisions:** Each turn requires you to invest, research, negotiate, or wage war. Choose wisely to build strength while undermining your opponents.
Getting Started
To begin your campaign:

Go to **PLAY LOCAL** from the main menu.
Review each country's starting **Economy, Military, and Resources** to pick a nation that matches your play style.
Use the **SETTINGS** menu to adjust difficulty and preferences before starting.
Global domination awaits, Commander.
